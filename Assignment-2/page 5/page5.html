Experiment - 1 (Volcano Erupting Fun)

list of supplies :

For making Volcano :

  • Empty plastic bottle (1-2 liter)
  • Cardboard or paper mache material

(newspaper strips, glue, water)

  • paint
  • tray or dish

For making Lava :

  • Baking soda (1/2)
  • white vineger (1/2)
  • Dish soap (1 tablespoon)
  • Red food colour
  • funnel

 

Safety:

  • Adult supervision
  • Goggles (optional)

Experiment steps:

  • Build the volcano :
  1. Cut the top off the plastic bottle to create a crater.
  2. Cover the bottle with cardboard or paper mache to create a volcano shape. Let it dry completely.
  3. Paint the volcano (optional).
  • Prepare the lava :
  1. In a separate container, mix the dish soap, vinegar, and food coloring (optional).
  • Erupt the volcano :
  1. place the volcano on the tray.
  2. pour a generous amount of baking soda into the crater.
  3. carefully pour the vinegar mixture into the crater using a funnel.
  4. observe the eruption!

 

Scientific explanation :

The fizzy eruption is caused by a chemical reaction between the baking soda (sodium bicarbonate) and the vinegar (acetic acid). This reaction produces carbon dioxide gas (CO2), which bubbles out rapidly, creating the lava flow and eruption. The dish soap helps the bubbles form and last longer.
